    Runara

    Noble Leader of Dragon's Rest

    A kind and benevolent spirit, Runara is the caretaker of Dragon's Rest. She serves the Dragon God Bahamut, Lord of the metallic dragons. She longs to see peace amongst the warring chromatic and metallic dragon factions.

    After explaining the recent quarrel of Sparkrender and Lord Aidron, Runara asked the gang for assistance in finding a crystal she can use to create a key to reconnect the bridge to the Observatory and rescue Lord Aidron.

    Tarak

    Botanist, Gardener & Bartender

    Tarak is an old, peaceful man. He abhors violence and brutality, and chooses to live out the twilight of his life in tranquility: gardening, cooking, brewing, and potion-making in Dragon's Rest.

    After losing contact with his friends, the Myconids, Tarak asks the gang if they can go find out what's wrong. He tells them of the guardian that blocks the entrance and gives them 3 Potions of Healing and 1 rotting bag of food to use as a peace-offering.

    Sparkrender

    Evil Blue Dragon

    Arrived on Stormwreck Isle a few months ago, attracted by the Draconic magic of the island. Recently fought and beat Lord Aidron and is imprisoning him at the Observatory.

    Myla

    Kobold & Town Tinkerer

    The most intelligent and crafty of the Kobolds, Myla is a brilliant blacksmith and tinkerer, able to create all sorts of items with what little tools she has.

    She is currently working on a Flident for Dorf.

    Lord Aidron

    Noble Bronze Dragon

    Noble Dragon that serves Runara and protects Dragon's Rest. Recently fought Sparkrender and lost. Currently imprisoned at the Observatory.

    Sharruth

    Ancient Evil Red Dragon

    Ancient Dragon inprisoned beneath the sea floor. His fiery grave created the island of Stormwreck Isle. There are legends of "Sharruth's Fury", volcanic fumes that boil up from the depths onto the surface of the island.
