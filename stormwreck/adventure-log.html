    Session 1 - Dragon's Rest and the Undead Octopus

      Party on the Beach

    • The gang washed up on the shore of Stormwreck Isle
    • Three zombified sea companions attacked the group
    • Dorf almost died, but was saved by Swan Jovi's Zombie Jacuzzi Disposal Method
    • Dorf kept the arm of one of the zombies????

      • Dragon's Rest

    • The gang made it to Dragon's Rest, where Dorf immediately insulted their leader
    • Runara gave the gang some info on Lord Aidron, Sparkrender, and the Conservatory
    • Dorf visited Myla the Tinkerer and commissioned the world's first ever Flident to be created
    • Swan visited the library and learned about Sharruth's Fury

      • Tarak's Quest

    • Conn visited the kitchen, immediately aggravating the town botanist Tarak by asking him to make poison
    • Tarak told the gang he can make them potions, but first they have to visit Seagrow Caves and find out whats up with the Myconids
    • Tarak gave the gang 3 Potions of Healing and one bag of rotting food

      • Seagrow Caves Octopus Battle

    • The gang visited Seagrow Caves, where they all were unsuccessful in not falling into the water
    • They were greeted by an Undead Octopus guarding the entrance
    • Conn blinded the already sight-impaired octopus with PAPRIKA!
    • Swan flew above the octopus, freezing a layer of the water and trapping all those involved
    • Dorf broke free with graceful spinning axe moves
    • Conn broke free to then dive back under the water
    • Blinded and restrained, what is an octopus to do?
    • Swan dropped hella stalactites on the octopus
    • Dorf rammed the stalactites deeper
    • Conn dealt the final blow with an underwater beak stab
    • Dorf lassoed a stalactite and climbed up the rope

      • The Voice

    • Floating spores in the air touched the heads of our companions, as they heard a desperate voice enter their minds. The voice told them that Seagrow Caves is not safe and they should leave immediately.
